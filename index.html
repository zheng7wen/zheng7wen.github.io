Qiwen Zheng      

My name is Qiwen Zheng, graduated from Hunan Normal University, June, 2022. My major is Electronic Information Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Leyong Jiang. My research interests focus on Weakly Supervised Learning, especially Weakly Supervised Object Localization (WSOL) and Semantic Segmentation (WSSS) . I also like to explore Unsupervised Learning and Vision + Language learning for object localization or semantic segmentation.
